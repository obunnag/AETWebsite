DAX NORMAN

Artist and Animator

About

Dax Norman is an artist and animator who teaches production courses where students work with 3-D animation, utilize software like Photoshop and create video games. As an artist, his work includes numerous paintings, animated projects and GIFS. Norman's work has been shown in exhibitions and festivals both nationally and internationally, such as Spike & Mike's Sick and Twisted Animation Festival and Gallery 444 in San Francisco. He's also created animation for Adult Swim and MTV.
