CAROLINA PEREZ

Sound Designer and Audio Engineer

About

Carolina Perez is a sound designer and audio engineer with experience in music production, film, theatre and interactive multimedia. She teaches AET classes in music technology and audio production, and she also works as a freelancer as a sound designer, audio engineer, music producer and composer. Perez holds an M.F.A. in Sound Design from the University of North Carolina School of the Arts and a B.M. in Music Technology from Florida International University.
