NEAL DAUGHERTY

2-D Artist and Designer

About

Neal Daugherty is an artist currently teaching courses that cover 2-D animation, working with Adobe Creative Cloud and a digital practicum that focuses in game concept and elements. His work outside of the classroom includes visual arts, graphic design and printmaking. Daugherty has worked with Nike and had his work featured in exhibitions, runs his own company called Misunderstood Technologies and is a part of the multimedia band, Death and Astronauts.
